Made for teachers to facilitate the coordination of team activities.

Classroom is a free online tool built to help teachers organise group activities.

Teachers can create a virtual class and invite students to join via email. Teachers can also send students priority invites for meeting and grouping. Grouping is made easy by giving students a fixed time to choose the group they want to be in.
